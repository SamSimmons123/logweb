Our story

The Silk Road Dispatch LLC was founded on one premise: to support and promote Owner/Operators. As a past Owner/Operator, we know what it is to run a trucking business, drive, and be away from your family. You spend your non-drive time looking for your next load, completing carrier packs, logging expenses and income, confirming you were paid, disbursing to your fuel card or bank account, making sure you file and report IFTA, MCS-150, UCR filings, and complete your Safety Audits. It is too much. You are taking away from your profits if your truck is idle while you perform all these tasks. As a Driver-you are the main link. It is YOU that keeps the freight moving YOU are the key. You need to keep your truck rolling to be profitable. Thats where we come in. We will secure freight and promote your Company.

We have a vast network of Brokers and Direct Shippers we have worked with for years, as well as the best real-time load boards to access freight specifically for you. We continue by dispatching, confirming appointments, completing check calls and submitting timely PODs and funding you are paid and off to the next load. Simply, through our network, we become your own personal office staff, dispatcher, bookkeeper, compliance manager, factoring agent, offering a credit line* to help get your business started, fuel card program, tire & roadside assistance program. We know what it takes. Our Everyday commitment to you! Improve the quality of your non-drive timeincrease your profit margins! Contact The Silk Road Dispatch, LLC today!
